Shipping Policy

At Oorla.com, we are committed to delivering your favorite Indian sweets and snacks to your doorstep with care and efficiency. Please read our shipping policy carefully to understand our delivery process.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 2-3 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

Note: During festival seasons (Diwali, Dussehra, etc.) processing time may extend to 5-7 business days due to high order volume.

Shipping Rates & Delivery Times

Shipping charges and delivery times vary based on your location and the shipping method selected at checkout:

Domestic Shipping (Within India):

• Standard Delivery: 5-7 business days - ₹99
• Express Delivery: 2-3 business days - ₹199
• Free shipping on orders above ₹1,500

International Shipping:

• USA & Canada: 7-12 business days - Starting at ₹999
• UK & Europe: 10-15 business days - Starting at ₹1,299
• Australia & New Zealand: 12-18 business days - Starting at ₹1,499
• Other Countries: 15-21 business days - Contact us for rates

Note: International shipping times are estimates and may vary due to customs clearance procedures.

Shipping Restrictions

Dairy Products:

Milk-based sweets (like Milk Cake, Kalakand, Peda) cannot be shipped to UK or Europe due to customs restrictions on dairy products.

Nut Products:

Products containing nuts (like Kaju Katli, Badam Barfi) cannot be shipped to UK due to import regulations on nut-based items.

Perishable Items:

Perishable items are carefully packaged with ice packs for domestic shipping. We recommend selecting express delivery for such items during summer months.

Customs, Duties & Taxes

International orders may be subject to import duties, customs fees, and taxes imposed by the destination country. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in our shipping costs.

Customers in UK, Canada, and Europe must pay applicable customs duties upon delivery. Please check with your local customs office for estimated duty amounts.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can track your package using the tracking number provided.

For domestic orders, tracking typically updates within 24 hours. For international orders, tracking may take 2-3 days to become active.

Delivery Issues

Please inspect your order upon delivery. If your package arrives damaged or if items are missing, please contact us at care@oorla.com within 24 hours with:

• Order number
• Photos of the damaged package/items
• Unboxing video (required for damage claims)

Failed Delivery Attempts

If delivery fails due to incorrect address, recipient unavailability, or refusal to accept the package, the order will be returned to us. Return shipping charges and a 20% restocking fee will apply for reshipment.

Shipping to Multiple Addresses

Currently, we can only ship to one address per order. If you need to send items to multiple locations, please place separate orders for each delivery address.

PO Box & Military Addresses

We ship to PO Box addresses within India only. For military addresses (APO/FPO), please contact us at care@oorla.com for special arrangements.

Special Occasions & Gift Delivery

We offer gift wrapping and personalized message cards for special occasions at no extra charge. Please mention your requirements in the order notes during checkout.

For time-sensitive deliveries (birthdays, anniversaries), we recommend placing your order at least 7 days in advance to ensure timely delivery.

Lost or Stolen Packages

Oorla is not responsible for lost or stolen packages confirmed delivered to the address entered for the order. Please ensure someone is available to receive the package or provide delivery instructions to the carrier.

If you believe your package is lost, please contact us immediately at care@oorla.com and we will work with the shipping carrier to locate your order.

Incorrect Address

Please ensure your shipping address is correct before completing your order. If you need to update your address after placing an order, contact us within 12 hours at care@oorla.com.

Address changes cannot be made once the order has been shipped. Any returned packages due to incorrect addresses will incur return shipping fees and restocking charges.

Packaging

We take extra care in packaging your sweets and snacks to ensure they reach you fresh and intact. All items are packed in food-grade materials with cushioning to prevent damage during transit.

Temperature-sensitive items are shipped with gel ice packs. For international orders, we use reinforced packaging to withstand long-distance shipping.
