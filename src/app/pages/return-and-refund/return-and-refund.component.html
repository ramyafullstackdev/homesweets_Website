Refund, Return & Cancellation Policy

Refund Policy

To get a refund, contact us at care@oorla.com. Once approved, refunds are processed within 48 hours and credited within 3–5 working days.

Note: All products sold on Oorla.com are non-returnable.

Note: An unboxing video is required for damage/missing item claims.

Note: Customers in Europe and Canada must pay duties.

Refund requests must be made only from the email used to place the order. Requests from a different email will not be prioritized.

Eligibility for Refund

Refund requests should be made within 24 hours of receiving the product. Only spoiled or damaged items are eligible.

Damages & Issues

If you receive a defective, damaged, or incorrect product, contact us immediately so we can evaluate the issue.

Fragile items like cookies and macaroons may break during transit. Refunds are not applicable for unpreventable breakage.

Customs may open parcels for inspection. We are not responsible for damages caused during customs checks.

Dairy Product Warning

Dairy products cannot be shipped to the UK or Europe. If ordered, we will offer an alternative or process a refund for those specific items.

Nuts Product Warning

Nut-based items cannot be shipped to the UK. We will offer an alternative or arrange a refund for those items.

How To Get a Refund?

Once we receive your request and approve it, your refund will be issued to your original payment method within 3–5 business days.

Note: Coupon difference refunds must be requested within 3 hours.

If the customer refuses delivery or does not respond to multiple attempts, no refund will be issued.

Return Policy

Returns are not accepted. For damaged items, email photo proof to care@oorla.com.

If items are not spoiled/damaged, returns will not be accepted. If items are returned, a 20% restocking fee applies.

Cancellation Policy

Orders placed between Oct 9–20 cannot be cancelled (Diwali season).

If we cancel your order due to stock or shipping issues, a full refund will be issued within 3–5 business days.

Cancellation requests should be emailed to care@oorla.com. Requests through social media or WhatsApp are not valid unless we respond there.

Duties & Clearance

Duties

Customers in Canada, UK, Europe, Sri Lanka, Thailand, Czech Republic, and certain Middle Eastern countries must pay duties. Oorla does not cover these charges.

Clearance

Countries like Australia, New Zealand, Spain, Portugal, France, Sri Lanka, Brazil, and Germany may require up to 20 days for customs clearance. We will keep you updated with tracking information.
