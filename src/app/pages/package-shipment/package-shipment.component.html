Package & Shipment

Learn how we handle packaging, dispatch, tracking, and delivery to ensure your orders reach you fresh and safely.

Secure Packaging

Every product is packed with industry-grade materials to ensure freshness, stability, and spill-proof safety. We use insulated boxes, moisture-safe wrapping, and double-sealed containers for sensitive food items.

Dispatch Process

Once your order is confirmed, our team prepares it for dispatch within 24 hours. All shipments are scanned, verified, and moved to the nearest logistics hub for forwarding.

Shipment Tracking

You will receive an SMS and email with your tracking ID immediately after dispatch. You can use this tracking link to monitor real-time updates on your order status from pickup to doorstep delivery.

Estimated Delivery Time

Delivery timelines differ by location:

  • Local Delivery: 1–2 Business Days
  • Within State: 2–4 Business Days
  • Rest of India: 4–7 Business Days

Delivery Issues

If your package is delayed, damaged, or appears tampered, contact our support team within 24 hours of delivery. A replacement or refund will be initiated after verification.
